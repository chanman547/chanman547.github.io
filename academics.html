What I learned from general electives:
  The courses that I have taken for general electives have helped me to think in a different way. To me it seems like engineering classes require you to think about problems in a certain perspective. When you get into general elective courses you have to think about problems differently than engineering problems. When you then go back to working on engineering problems you consider the way that you thought about problems in the general elective courses and also the way you think of engineering problems.

How general electives influence engineering:
  One instance I can think of when I used what I learned in general education electives was in my SE 409 class. For that class we had a project where we had to work on requirements elicitation. One thing that we had to conisder was different peoples perspectives. This is something that I have also talked about in general elective courses. When general education classes and engineering classes are mixed it has a profound impact on innovation on every level. It helps those who are making decisions about products to conisder every aspect of the project not just the technical aspects.
